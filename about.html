About Us

At CC Records we celebrate the timeless joy of discovering music on vinyl. From flipping through records to studying album credits, we strive to recreate the magic of crate-digging in a modern, digital space.

Our store transforms your online shopping journey into a vibrant exploration. Every record page offers rich details about the album, from the artists to the producers and everyone who brought the music to life. With just a click, you can dive deeper into their contributions and explore other records they've worked on, making every visit to CC Records a discovery.

For collectors and superfans alike, we provide tools to expand your vinyl library beyond the hits—whether it’s tracking down rare features or uncovering hidden gems. With CC Records, every album is more than just music; it's a story waiting to be spun on your turntable.
